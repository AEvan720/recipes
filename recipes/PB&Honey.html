Peanut Butter & Honey

Starting with a simple classic

Ingredients

  • Your favorite peanut butter
  • Your favorite honey
  • Your bread of choice

    • Steps

    1. Take out the amount of slices you want. Obviously a traditional sandwich is 2 slices, but if you're running low on bread you can take a single slice and fold it in half at the end
    2. Next we add the peanut butter to one side of each slice or the single slice. As a PB lover, I make sure to cover every inch I can.
    3. It's honey time. I prefer a figure 8 pattern on each slice aiming for the middle. You want to be careful putting honey towards the corners where it can leak out
    4. Finally it's time to close our sandwich. If you're using 2 slices, place one on top of the other so the peanut butter sides are facing each other. If you are only using one slice, simply fold it in half.
